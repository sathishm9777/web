What is a car service?

A car service is like a routine check-up but for your vehicle. It might be as intensive as a 50-point checklist or can be a simple service and repair that takes care of the things susceptible to wear and tear. Its importance stems from the fact that your safety is largely dependent on your car. While you may experience the discomfort while driving and can fix the issues like tyre puncture and oil change, it is only a professional who can actually pin-point the problem and repair it.So the next time the engine light flashes, bring your car to Acton Service Centre to get the help it requires.In this article, we provide a comprehensive car servicing guide, ensuring you know everything before you drive to a service centre.
